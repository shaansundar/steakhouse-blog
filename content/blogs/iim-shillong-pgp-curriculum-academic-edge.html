  • Strategic Management: Focusing on formulating and implementing business strategies for competitive advantage.

The pedagogy for these core courses often involves a blend of case studies, simulations, group projects, and interactive lectures, encouraging critical thinking and collaborative problem-solving. A flagship program, the IIM Shillong rural immersion program, is a mandatory component where students spend several days in rural communities. This program aims to expose future managers to the ground realities of rural India, understand socio-economic challenges, and identify opportunities for sustainable development and inclusive growth. Students work on live projects, interact with local communities, entrepreneurs, and government officials, gaining invaluable insights into grassroots management and social responsibility. This experience is critical for developing empathy and a broader perspective on business's role in society. It's a unique aspect that helps students understand the Indian context beyond urban settings.

The Himalayan Outbound Program (HOP)

Another distinctive module is the Himalayan Outbound Program, which focuses on leadership development, team building, and personal resilience. Students undertake challenging treks and outdoor activities in the Himalayan terrain, pushing their physical and mental limits. This program is designed to foster camaraderie, enhance decision-making under pressure, and build a strong sense of teamwork – qualities essential for effective leadership in any business environment. It's an intense experience that complements the academic rigor with practical leadership challenges.
